Save Our Sea Turtles

Plastic Pollution

Turtles mistake plastic bags for jellyfish and other food. This can block their digestive system and cause starvation.

Learn More →

Nesting Beaches

Coastal development and artificial lights scare away nesting mothers and confuse hatchlings, leading them away from the ocean.

Learn More →

Fishing Bycatch

Turtles get accidentally caught in fishing nets and lines (bycatch), preventing them from surface breathing and causing drowning.
